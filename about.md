I’m Ritvik Diada, a student at Bridgewater-Raritan High School exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and human ethics. I started **AI Oversight** because while everyone is talking about how fast AI is moving, not enough people are asking where it's taking us.

### My Motivation
It’s easy to think of algorithms as neutral math, but they’re built by people with their own biases. When a piece of software helps decide who gets a job or which college application stands out, it isn't just "data" anymore. It’s a decision that changes someone's life. 

I’m fascinated by the power of these tools, but I'm also worried about the "black box" problem where we can't really explain why an AI makes the choices it does. This blog is my way of documenting what I’m learning and trying to figure out how we can keep technology human-centric.
